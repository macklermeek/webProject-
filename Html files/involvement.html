Get Involved with Our Fraternity

Community Service

Delta Sigma Phi helps our community with their adopt-a-highway cleanups, post homecoming street cleanup and support for the humane society

Philanthropy

Delta Sigma Phi's national philanthropy is the American Red Cross, and Eta Omicrons local philanthropy is the Humane Society

Brotherhood

Delta Sigma Phi is a Brothership for life that connects you to the 125,000 members that have come before you

Leadership Opportunities

Being in a fraternity allows for the development of leadership skills as well as the development as your personal character

Campus Involvement

Greek life is a largely social group and joining a fraternity connects you to a large and supporting community on campus

Nationals

The National connection allows us to connect with many people from all over as well at leadership training seminars, national convention and career development workshops
